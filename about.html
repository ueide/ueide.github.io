About

Ueide is a Brazilian UX Designer, currently living in Amsterdam.

Since his childhood, he loved to question himself and learn a little about everything, from this curiosity also came the desire to share his learning, to give information in an easy way for everyone to understand. He found these two passions in UX Design, learning about something that is constantly evolving as well as looking for the best way to bring information to the public.

During his career he worked for 7 years as a physical education tutor, during that time he developed important skills such as communication, leadership, collaboration and problem solving.

In her free time, she loves to play sports, visit places, enjoy nature and read about works of fiction.
