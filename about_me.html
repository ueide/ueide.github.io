About me

I worked as a UX UI designer on social projects. I had the pleasure of working on a project entirely focused on digital accessibility, it's a topic that I like, and I've been improving myself.

I have experience with different UX tools like CRO (conversion rate optimization), mobile and desktop interfaces, qualitative and quantitative research methodologies, information architecture, heuristic analysis, user journey mapping, wireframes, prototyping, usability testing, and more. Seeking to deliver user-centered design solutions, I use specific tools for the needs of each project.

Before UX Design, I worked as a professor in the health field in Brazil, where I helped more than 700 students and realized scientific research related to health and sports.

I'm comfortable collaborating in brainstorming ideas, cooperating between teams and solving problems.

I like playing games, playing sports and learning about cultures in my spare time. These activities bring me professional inspiration.
