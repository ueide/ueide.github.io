About me

I'm a UX designer based in Amsterdam, NL. I specialise in designing interactive end-to-end projects for mobile and desktop interfaces. In my last education background, I studied psychology and human behaviour, which has given me an analytical vision and the skills to connect with people.

As a UX/UI designer, I work with a solid discovery process, identifying opportunities, informing decision-making, and documenting the entire process to engage stakeholders. I have hands-on experience in all aspects of the design process, working on designing interactive end-to-end projects mobile (iOS and Android) and desktop interfaces, holding experience with wireframes and prototypes (low, mid and high fidelity), user research, usability testing, user journey, information architecture and more.

Before UX Design, I worked as a professor in the health field in Brazil, where I helped more than 700 students and realised scientific research related to health and sports.
